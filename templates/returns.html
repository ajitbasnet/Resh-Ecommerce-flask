Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Items must be returned in the same condition as received.

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@solestyle.com
  2. Receive your return authorization and prepaid shipping label
  3. Package your items securely with original packaging
  4. Attach the prepaid label and drop off at any authorized location

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability. Exchange requests must be made within 30 days of purchase.

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned items. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items worn or damaged by customer
  • Items without original packaging
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Sale items marked as final sale

Contact Us

For questions about returns or exchanges, contact us at returns@solestyle.com or call (555) 123-4567.
