Size Guide

How to Measure Your Feet

  1. Place a piece of paper on the floor against a wall
  2. Step on the paper with your heel against the wall
  3. Mark the longest part of your foot on the paper
  4. Measure the distance from the wall to the mark
  5. Repeat for both feet and use the larger measurement

Brand-Specific Sizing Notes

Nike

Nike shoes typically run true to size. For running shoes, consider going up 0.5 size for extra comfort.

Adidas

Adidas shoes generally run true to size. Ultraboost models may run slightly small.

Puma

Puma shoes typically run true to size. Some lifestyle models may run slightly large.

New Balance

New Balance offers various width options. Most models run true to size.

Converse

Converse shoes typically run large. Consider going down 0.5 to 1 full size.

Sizing Tips

  • Measure your feet in the evening when they're at their largest
  • Always measure both feet and use the larger measurement
  • Consider the type of socks you'll wear with the shoes
  • If you're between sizes, generally go with the larger size
  • Remember that different brands may fit differently
